#7. Spring Break

Over spring break, in a short summary: I went to Dallas and was injured, a lot. It was fun i guess, i visted my cousins, aunt, and Dan. (Dan is my aunt's spouse and i forgot what that was called so he's just Dan)

We did go to a science museum which is where i got the scar currently on my wrist (Lawsuit against the museum people pending.) I also may have contracted Salmonella and destroyed my ankles jumping off of random stuff. That was fun
